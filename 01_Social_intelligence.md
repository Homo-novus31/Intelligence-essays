#  Social Intelligence > SuperIntelligence

Originally published on Substack titled - Social not Super.

---

## The Problem

Modern AI systems are trained in isolation.

They optimize for individual problem-solving, scale, and reward functions.

But they do not interact, negotiate, or evolve socially.

This is strange.

Because human intelligence itself emerged from **interaction**, not isolation.

So the real question is:

> If our intelligence is social… why are we building AI alone?

---

##  Human Intelligence is Social

The human brain has not significantly evolved in 100,000 years.

Yet in that time, we built civilizations, science, and technology.

This progress did not come from individual intelligence alone.

It came from:
- Shared ideas  
- Arguments  
- Collaboration  

Intelligence is not just stored in individuals.  
It is **distributed across interactions**.

---

## The Limitation of Current AI

Current agentic AI systems rely on:
- Reward optimization  
- Isolated environments  
- Narrow objectives  

They lack:
- Context awareness  
- Social understanding  
- Consequence modeling  

As a result:

Even highly capable systems struggle in real-world environments  
where interactions are dense, dynamic, and unpredictable.

They require constant human supervision.

Not because they are weak.

But because they are **alone**.

---

##  The Shift Toward Social AI

A new direction is emerging.

Instead of scaling individual models, we explore:

> **Interaction between agents**

Frameworks like:
- Adaptive Theory of Mind (A-ToM)  
- Multi-agent environments (SOTOPIA, CAMEL)  
- Social reasoning benchmarks (SOCIALIQA, ToMI)  

…all point toward one idea, the future is Social-AI:

**Intelligence improves through interaction.**

---

##  Real-World Implications

### Healthcare
Current Healthcare Systems:
-Lack coordination between doctors, records of patients and pharmacists.
-AI is mostly non-existent in this current equation.
AI agents coordinating across:
- Diagnosis  
- Treatment  
- Monitoring  

→ Creating a unified, intelligent system instead of isolated tools, transforming Healthcare into a coordinated intelligent system. 

---

### Education
Current Education:
-Has a wide gap between teachers and AI tutors.
-AI tutors being mostly generalised fail to understand each studentas per his strengths and weaknesses'
AI that:
- Interacts with teachers  
- Adapts to students  
- Learns from feedback loops  

→ Moving from static tutoring to dynamic learning systems.

---

###  Logistics
Current Logistics:
-Is unpredictable and in constant chaos bcause of the ever changing consumer sentiment.
- This leads to delays and shortages in some places while surplus in others'
Multi-agent coordination can:
- Predict demand  
- Adapt supply chains  
- Reduce chaos in real-time  

→ From reactive systems → to intelligent coordination.

---

##  Key Insight

We are scaling intelligence.

But ignoring interaction.

That is the bottleneck.

---

##  Final Thought

We are not building superintelligence.

We are building **isolated intelligence**.

And isolated intelligence does not scale into the real world.

The next breakthrough will not come from bigger models.

It will come from **connected ones**.
